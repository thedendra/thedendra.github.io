High Quality, Sustainable, Smart Home Automation.

Meet the perfection.

Our products are designed to offer you the best experience possible. Discover the next-gen automation system for your home, made directly by our local industries with our own materials. No importing, no threats. With our setups you have the full control of every single part of your house, with the best stability and durability you could fond in the market.

Light as the light.

So, what's a footprint? We actually know, our products don't. We use only 100% sustainable materials, like recycled ones or ones from sustainable production. Our OSes have ultra-green settings, with the capability of super energy saving working with every kind of environment. Less waste, less pollution, less cost. A simple step that makes the difference both for you and for the environment.

House at a whisper.

Lock a door, switch on a lamp, set a timer for the cake. The age has changed: nothing will stop you from having the power on every single corner of your house. All of this, just with a basic system. Old house? Don't worry, certain times also old problems require modern solution, and we're the answer. Here's another point in the list of why you should choose us.

Stability? We got you that.

Woah! Heard that? 10GB/s. Of course you can't, but sometimes also the most hidden things make the difference. Get everything you want right where and when you want, with the lowest latency in this world. Make each of your plan go at its best, either if it's a laser party or a simple morning alarm. Guess what? File transfer and cloud. Yes, we did it. This speed applies also on this job. Not convinced yet? Keep reading!

We use Linux, btw.

Big projects need stable roots, they said. Here's the point. Have you ever wondered how to centralize speed, stability, security, smoothness, versatility or even durability? Linux. The answer is Linux. We fork our OSes' kernel directly from their main repositories, with the support of the Arch community. Nothing will stop you from exploring. No errors can be made, it's just simple as that. No one can steal what's yours, of course.
