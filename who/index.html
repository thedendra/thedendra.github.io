Δένδρα

We are Alice, Ginevra, Giovanni and Davide, four teenagers who wanted to develop a completely eco-sustainable Home Automation System. We called our project “Dendra”, which in ancient Greek means “tree”. Trees are the source of life of our planet, our planet is our home and that’s why we must respect it starting from the sustainability of our houses. We used to consider pollution as a phenomenon outside our home, thinking about smog, deforestation and air and water pollution. Most people do not know that our houses pollute 7 times more than our cars!
