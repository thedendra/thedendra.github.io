A Look to the Market Request

Our company will take part in a rapidly growing market: the Home Automation one. Researching some data, we found out that only the 1.5% of the businesses of this kind aim to provide a fully sustainable ecosystem. Where the world lacks, Δένδρα tries to fill the gaps.

Our materials: Treeplast©

Then the promise, now the action. We just discovered the future: Treeplast®. Wondering how to get plastic materials with zero impact, we found this project, which main objective is to eliminate plastic pollution by using only cellulose, lignin and natural resins, recreating the texture of plastic.
