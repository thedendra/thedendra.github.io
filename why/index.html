Making the world a better place? Challenge accepted.

Δένδρα

Our world is changing, and our habits must do the same thing. We don’t have to be superheroes, we can just start from our everyday reality: our houses. Both in production and consumption, our products aim to satisfy some of our agenda 2030’s objectives, the only way for us to change the game.

Objective #7

Our power is perfectly balanced, as everything should be. With our softwares, all the temperatures and electricity consumption are under control, managed in order to use the minimum quantity of resources possible without you noticing. We use only energy from renewable sources making our products.

Objectives #13, #14 and #15

All of our materials are sustainable or from sustainable sources. We work hard to prevent the plastic pollution in the seas and on land, using only recycled polyethylene and donating all of the savings to organisations such as Greenpeace, which aim is to protect the world and its environment.

Objectives #5 and #10

Last but not least, we’ll offer workplaces for everyone, without discrimination by gender, age, etnicity, religion, financial situation or by any other distinctive feature. We aim to create a collaborative organization through different people, increasing motivation and life standards. With our savings we take action in particular in filling the gender gap, helping foundations such as AIDOS.
